## Update Rules

**Adding a document:** Add an entry under the appropriate category. All fields are required. Add new tags to the Tag Glossary if the document covers a subject area not yet tagged.

**Adding a category:** Add a new Category block. Keep categories organized by research domain, not by article.

**Updating tags:** Add to the Tag Glossary. Do not rename existing tags — other documents reference them. If a tag's scope changes, update the "Covers" description.

**Do not:** Add analysis, interpretation, or relevance judgments to document entries. The "Covers" field describes content, not importance. The series map determines which documents are primary/supporting for each article — the research index does not make relevance judgments.
