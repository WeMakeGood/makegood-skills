## Update Rules

**Adding an article:** Copy the Article [N] block. All fields are required except Cross-Domain Connections (populate after adjacent articles exist).

**Updating status:** Change the Status fields only. Do not modify other fields unless the article's structural role has changed.

**Modifying an article's payload:** Update What This Article Carries, Argument Arc, and Thesis Elements together — they are interdependent. Check whether changes affect adjacent articles' Cross-Domain Connections.

**Do not:** Add sections not in this template. Do not add draft content, editorial notes, or process observations. Do not restructure the document. The drafting skill reads specific fields by name — invented sections are invisible to it and clutter the file for maintaining agents.
